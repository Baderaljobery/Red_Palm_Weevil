## ü™≤ Background: What is the Red Palm Weevil?
The Red Palm Weevil (Rhynchophorus ferrugineus) is widely regarded as the most devastating pest affecting palm trees globally. Native to Southeast Asia, this invasive beetle poses a critical threat to date palms and the agricultural sector.

The primary danger lies in its larval stage, where the weevil bores into the palm's trunk, destroying the internal vascular system and structural integrity of the tree. Often undetected until severe damage has occurred, infestations frequently lead to the collapse and death of the palm, causing significant economic losses for farmers and threatening regional food security.
